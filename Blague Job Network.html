Blague Job Network: Personalized Path to Professional Growth

Date: June - July 2023

Skills: Website Design, User Experience, User Interface Design

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator

Check The Figma

Context:

The entire product design was personally crafted by me, aiming to create an intuitive and user-centric job-finding platform.

User-Centric Interface Design：

During the design process, I engaged with job seekers to understand the intricacies of their search experience. This led to the creation of a website interface that:

Minimizes the complexity of finding niche opportunities by highlighting personalized job recommendations.Offers a seamless and intuitive navigation experience, enabling users to effortlessly discover roles that align with their career path.Engages users with interactive elements that make job searching more than a mere task, but an informative journey into the job market.

Visual Aesthetics:

The visual design of Blague Job Network was conceptualized to be as dynamic and vibrant as the users it serves. Starting with a clean and minimalist foundation, I introduced a gradient color scheme dominated by purples and blues, infusing a sense of energy and optimism into the interface. The choice of colors and the fluid design elements reflect the innovative spirit of the platform, inviting users into a space where their career potential can be fully realized.

As a personal design endeavor, Blague Job Network is the embodiment of my vision for a smarter job search platform, one that not only connects users to their next opportunity but also empowers and inspires their professional journey.
